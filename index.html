How it works

Show up curious. Leave with a sharper mental model.

  1. 01

    Pick a topic

    A guest selects a paper, system, or theme — usually something they've shipped or studied closely.

  2. 02

    High-level tutorial

    ~30 minutes of context, intuition, and the key technical ideas — no slide-deck dogma required.

  3. 03

    Open discussion

    We dig into limitations, future directions, and how it fits into the broader ML stack.
